# 20 — Glossary

Canonical definitions of terms used throughout this documentation. Where a Danish or EU statutory term exists, it is given in *italics* in parentheses.

| Term                                          | Definition                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Equipment**                                 | Generic X2 entity representing a physical asset. In Vehicle Trade, always a road vehicle.                                                                                                             |
| **Trade Equipment**                           | An Equipment row that represents a vehicle the workshop has bought (or intends to buy) for resale. Identified by a system-generated code (e.g. `EQ000001`).                                           |
| **Customer Equipment / Customer Vehicle**     | An Equipment row that represents a vehicle owned by a workshop *customer* (typically keyed by registration number). Created separately from any Trade Equipment record, even after a sale.            |
| **Equipment Status**                          | Lifecycle state of a trade equipment row: *Draft*, *Stock*, or *Sold*.                                                                                                                                |
| **Job Card**                                  | Record of work performed on an Equipment. The Job Card's "customer" is the workshop while the vehicle is in stock; it becomes the buyer once the vehicle has been sold (non-warranty work only).      |
| **Product**                                   | Catalogue item that backs purchase and sales document lines. Carries SKU, description, price, VAT classification, and (optionally) inventory tracking.                                                |
| **Equipment Inventory Product** (`BILLAG-xx`) | Inventory-tracked product whose lots represent vehicles in stock. One SKU per purchase-tax classification.                                                                                            |
| **Equipment Sales Product** (`BIL-xx`)        | Non-inventory product representing the *sales* tax classification of a vehicle (private, business, export, etc.).                                                                                     |
| **Lot**                                       | Inventory sub-record tracking an individual identifiable item. For Vehicle Trade the Lot type is always *Serial*, quantity is 0 or 1, and the serial number is the equipment code.                    |
| **Inventory**                                 | Aggregated stock-on-hand and value for an Equipment Inventory Product, summed across its lots.                                                                                                        |
| **Purchase Type**                             | Classification that determines how a vehicle's purchase and subsequent sale are taxed and valued. See [Purchase types](../40-purchase-types/README.md).                                               |
| **Margin Scheme** (*brugtmomsordningen*)      | Special Danish VAT scheme for second-hand goods (Momsloven §§69–71) where VAT is charged only on the dealer's profit margin, not the full sale price. The standard treatment for used cars bought from private citizens. |
| **EU Acquisition** (*EU-erhvervelse*)         | Cross-border B2B purchase from a VAT-registered seller in another EU member state. Reverse charge applies; the buyer self-accounts for Danish acquisition VAT.                                        |
| **New Means of Transport**                    | EU VAT category for vehicles under 6 months old or under 6,000 km. Always taxed in the country of destination regardless of seller status.                                                            |
| **Registreringsafgift**                       | Danish vehicle registration tax, levied on first registration of a vehicle in Denmark. Not VAT; not deductible. Materially affects valuation of imported vehicles.                                    |
| **DMR / Motorregister**                       | The Danish Motor Vehicle Register. Authoritative source for vehicle identity and registration status.                                                                                                 |
| **Afregningsbilag**                           | Self-billing settlement note. Standard Danish instrument when a VAT-registered buyer (the workshop) buys from a non-VAT-registered seller (a private citizen). Modelled in this system as a sales credit note. |
